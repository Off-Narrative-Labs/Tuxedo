Expand description

A means of checking that an output can be redeemed (aka spent). This check is made on a per-output basis and neither knows nor cares anything about the verification logic that will be applied to the transaction as a whole. Nonetheless, in order to avoid malleability, we we take the entire stripped and serialized transaction as a parameter.
